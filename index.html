About Us

Welcome to Malagos Garden Resort, your nature escape nestled in the highlands of Davao City. Surrounded by lush greenery and fresh mountain air, our 12-hectare inland nature-themed resort is a sanctuary for families, adventurers, and nature lovers alike. We are proud to be a homegrown, family-run destination that celebrates the rich biodiversity, culture, and heritage of Mindanao.

SUSTAINABLE

Rooted in sustainability and local pride, Malagos is more than just a place to stay — it's a space to reconnect with the earth, with loved ones, and with yourself. We are proud stewards of a working farm, a chocolate-making destination, and a sanctuary for flora and fauna, all while promoting mindful tourism and environmental care.

FUN FOR
EVERYONE

Whether you're here for a family getaway, a peaceful staycation, or a day of exploration, Malagos Garden Resort offers something for everyone. Enjoy interactive farm and animal encounters, tree-to-bar chocolate tours, and relaxing walks through our serene gardens — all designed to create meaningful, hands-on experiences in nature.

A MYRIAD OF
ACTIVITIES

Malagos Garden Resort is packed with fun and unique experiences for visitors of all ages. You can join our tree-to-bar workshop where you get to make your own chocolate straight from cacao beans. Meet and interact with friendly animals at the petting zoo, take a relaxing walk through our beautiful scenic gardens, or explore the resort on a guided farm tour to learn more about sustainable farming practices. Whether you're here for a peaceful day surrounded by nature or looking for hands-on adventures, Malagos Garden Resort has something exciting and memorable waiting for you.

At Malagos Garden Resort, we invite you to slow down, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and experience the beauty of nature, culture, and connection all in one place.
